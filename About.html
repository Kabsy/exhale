    About Us.


    The Institute

    Exhale Vocational Training Institute is an innovative and highly enriched state of the art learning community located in Kaduna State, Nigeria. We are a Federal Ministry of Education and NAtional Board for Technical Education (NBTE) Approved and Recognized education provider with knowledge and staff resources of the highest standards.


    At Exhale, we seek to realize the potentials of our students by providing challenge, encourage critical thinking, promoting teamwork and developing leadership capabilities. It is our aim to get the best in every individual. There is a sense of purpose within the Institute, built on an atmosphere that is stimulating, friendly and supportive.

    Our Approach

    We are aware the world as we know it is rapidly changing, requiring new skills and embracing evolving technology. Exhale Training Institute is therefore committed to providing teachers and students with powerful new tools and training that enables personalized, student-centered learning by blending the best of face to face instruction with new technology for each student to reach their full intellectual and social potential. Our students are thus prepared to respond to the challenges and opportunities in our 21st century


    Exhale is a reputable tutorial school for developing successful learners where we incoporate a rich and diverse curriculum that prepares students for the following exams; SSCE, WAEC, NECO and JAMB.


    Our unique approach to education transforms these young ones into students ready for higher education both in Nigeria and in any part of the world.
